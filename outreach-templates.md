## Notes on sending these at scale

- Personalize at least the first line of every message — a copy-pasted opener is the fastest way to get ignored or marked spam.
- Space outreach out; don't message 50 group admins in one day from the same account.
- Track what you send in a simple spreadsheet (site/creator name, date contacted, response, link live y/n) so you're not re-pitching the same person twice.
